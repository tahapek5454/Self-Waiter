**Self-Waiter** is a digital order management platform designed to streamline and enhance the ordering process in cafes and restaurants. With this system, customers can place orders directly from their smartphones or tablets without leaving their seats. When the order is ready, customers receive a notification and can either collect the order themselves or have it delivered to their table by a waiter.
